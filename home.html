Introduction

This is a platform that facilitates language exchange based on shared interests. It pairs you with other users that have the same or similar hobbies and likes.

By creating an account, you will be able to search other people who knows other languages and become a pair. A pair teaches each other their own language and learn the pther person's language. You can also talk about your hobbies and have fun!

Developped by: Rui Fei Mo, Qihao Xiao, Ziye Zhou.
